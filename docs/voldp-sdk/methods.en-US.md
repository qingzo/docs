## Events

Auto-subscribed: most common events.

Manually subscribed:

- `窗口即将关闭`
- `即将导航`
- `请求新窗口`
- `下载开始`
- `文件拖入`

Interceptable events return:

- `1` Intercept
- `0` Allow
